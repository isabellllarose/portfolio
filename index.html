About

A bit about me.

I'm currently completing the Advanced Diploma of Building Design (Architectural) at Holmesglen, working across residential and commercial design, BIM workflows, construction documentation and design presentation.

I'm drawn to the full process of a project — from reading a site and understanding a brief, through to the details that make a building legible on paper and resolved in reality. I find the most interesting work happens in that space between design intent and technical documentation, where decisions have real consequences.

This portfolio is a living document — it collects my academic projects alongside anything I work on going forward, and it'll grow as I do.
